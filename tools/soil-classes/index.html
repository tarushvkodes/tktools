The 12 orders of Soil Taxonomy (USDA Standard).

Alfisols

Moderately leached, high fertility, often under forests.

Andisols

Formed in volcanic ash, very high water capacity.

Aridisols

Dry soils of deserts, calcium carbonate accumulation.

Entisols

Young soils, little development, common on floodplains.

Gelisols

Permafrost within 2m, cold climates.

Histosols

Organic soils, bogs and peat lands.
